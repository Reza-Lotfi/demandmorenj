We need to Demand More now

In Colorado and Kentucky, we had incredible Progressive momentum that seemed to be enough to elect Charles Booker and Andrew Romanoff. However, we saw big money funnel into those races and put them out of reach. The only way we can be sure of a Progressive victory on July 7th is by joining our movement and hitting the phones so we can guarantee that New Jersey elects a Progressive majority.

VOLUNTEER NOW

Demand More Fair Elections

New Jersey’s primary election ballot is designed to make it difficult for voters to find insurgent candidates and gives party endorsed candidates (typically incumbents) an unfair advantage. Report: Ballot Design Unfair Preference to Incumbents!

Demand More COVID-19 Relief

One stimulus check was not enough. We need to get cash directly into the hands of working people, cancel rent, and ensure that recently unemployed workers retain health insurance.

Demand More Affordable Housing

Accelerated by the COVID-19 crisis, our country is barreling towards another housing crisis because millions of people across the country are unable to pay rent. We need to make a massive investment in affordable housing and guarantee housing as a human right. John Oliver: COVID-19 is Causing an Affordable Housing Crisis

Demand More Green Jobs

At a time in which millions of people are unemployed, it’s clear that we need to make a significant investment in a Green New Deal, to build the sustainable world of the future and create a Federal Jobs Guarantee. AOC: Our Future with a Green New Deal

Demand More Medicare for All

We are seeing, in real time, the catastrophic shortcomings of the existing, employer-based insurance model. Millions of people are uninsured or underinsured and do not have access to the services they need. We need to guarantee healthcare as a human right.
